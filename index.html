What is Open Auto Transport?

Open auto transport is the most common and cost-effective method for shipping vehicles across the country. It involves loading your car onto an open carrier, typically seen on highways, where it is securely fastened for the journey. Unlike enclosed transport, which provides full protection from the elements, open transport exposes vehicles to weather conditions like rain, sun, or dust. However, it remains a safe and reliable option for most vehicles, particularly standard sedans, SUVs, trucks, and motorcycles.

Open auto transport is ideal for those prioritizing affordability over premium protection. It’s perfect for daily drivers or vehicles that don’t require the extra shielding provided by enclosed trailers. AmeriFreight ensures that their open carriers are operated by highly rated, insured professionals, guaranteeing your vehicle’s safety throughout its journey.

The benefits of open auto transport include:

  • Cost-Effectiveness: Open transport is typically 40-60% cheaper than enclosed transport, making it a budget-friendly choice for most vehicle owners.
  • Wide Availability: Open carriers are more common, allowing for faster scheduling and pickup times compared to enclosed options.
  • Versatility: Suitable for a wide range of vehicles, from compact cars to larger SUVs, trucks, and even motorcycles.
  • Reliable Security: Despite exposure to the elements, vehicles are securely fastened to prevent movement during transit, ensuring safety.

While open transport may not be suitable for high-value or delicate vehicles like classic or exotic cars, it’s an excellent choice for most car owners looking for a balance between cost and safety. AmeriFreight’s expertise ensures that even with open transport, your vehicle is handled with the utmost care.
